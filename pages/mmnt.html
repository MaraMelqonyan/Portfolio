Moment Platform

OVERVIEW

MMNT is platform that unifies and simplifies the photography market. Client can find, choose and reserved a photo session in a city for any date at any price..

Photographer can sell his services no matter from his location and future plans. He simply offers “a listing” for specific dates in a city for a specific price.

Understanding the problem

Even before MMNT hired a product designer, a beta version of the new platform was implemented. These layouts were created without any usability testing and had little regard for the technical and product limitations of the scope of work. I conducted research interviews with our main users (account managers) to identify any pain points they encountered while using the beta.

Wireframing the solution

I quickly modeled a few basic wireframes to gather feedback from product, developers, and users about the overall layout and structure of the wizard form. This included creating a standardized visual hierarchy and layout for the future wizard component.
